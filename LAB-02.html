what is cyber security

  • Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, theft, or damage.
  • It involves technologies, processes, and policies designed to keep information safe from hackers, malware, phishing, and other cyber threats.
  • WHAT ARE CYBER THREATS
    • Phishing
    • Malware
    • Data branches
    • Denial of services
    • Identity theft

PHISHING

  • Fake emails or websites tricking users into revealing sensitive information
  • Example: “Your account is locked, click here to reset.”

Malware

  • Malicious software such as viruses, worms, or trojans.
  • Can steal, delete, or encrypt data

Data Breaches

  • A data breach is an incident where sensitive, confidential, or protected information is accessed, stolen, or exposed without authorization

Denial of Service (DoS/DDoS)

  • Flooding systems with traffic to make them unavailable
  • Often used to shut down websites or services

Identity Theft

  • Stealing personal data to impersonate someone
  • Leads to fraud and damaged reputation

CYBER VIDEO

Cybersecurity Overview

Category Description Examples
Defination Practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from cyberattacks Also called Information Security, IT Security, or Electronic Information Security
Main Areas Different domains of cybersecurity Network Security: Protects computer networks from intruders
- Application Security: Secures apps/software
- Information Security: Safeguards data integrity & privacy
- Cloud Security: Protects cloud-based systems
- Endpoint Security: Secures devices like laptops & phones
Common Threats Types of attacks that target individuals and organizations Phishing: Fake emails/websites trick users into revealing data
- Malware: Harmful software (viruses, worms, ransomware)
- Ransomware: Encrypts data until ransom is paid
- Data Theft: Stealing sensitive information
- AI-powered Attacks: Emerging threat using automation & AI
Best Practices Steps to improve cybersecurity Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Keep software updated
- Avoid suspicious links/emails
- Backup data regularly
Enterprise Focus Cybersecurity as part of risk management Organizations integrate cybersecurity into overall risk management strategies
